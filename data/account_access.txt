Account lockouts occur after five consecutive failed login attempts per company policy.

To unlock an account: verify user identity via employee ID or manager confirmation, then unlock the account in Active Directory. Never unlock without proper identity verification.

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) failures are typically caused by time-sync issues on authenticator apps. Ask the user to sync their authenticator app time and retry.

For Single Sign-On (SSO) errors: clear the browser cache, try an incognito window, or attempt a different supported browser (Chrome or Edge preferred).

New employee account provisioning takes up to 24 hours after HR submits the onboarding request. Expedited provisioning requires manager approval via the IT portal.

If a user is locked out of email specifically, check the mailbox quota in Exchange Admin Center. Full mailboxes block logins. Archive or delete old emails to restore access.
